Welcome to Primary Health

We build software to help independent primary care providers run their practice. We can help independent primary care doctors practice value-based care for their Medicaid patients.

Originally from the Bronx, we wanted to find a way to improve primary care for Medicaid populations in New York. By helping providers meet quality goals, generating reports, and automatically certifying them for government programs, we help practices earn more revenue per year while also reducing burnout.

We saw first-hand how underserved and bogged down independent practices are as we spent months building software for our family's private practice in New York City.
